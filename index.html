About the Symposium

Where sunlight and hydrogen
meet a sustainable future.

A single day bringing together international groups working on photovoltaics, photodetectors and solar-driven fuels — from device physics and interfacial engineering to the chemistry of clean energy conversion.

01

Advanced Solar Cells

Perovskite, organic and tandem architectures — pushing efficiency, stability and scalable manufacturing for next-generation photovoltaics.

02

Photophysics & Interfaces

Charge extraction, grain boundaries and interfacial energetics that govern how these materials capture and move energy.

03

Solar Fuels & Hydrogen

Photoanodes, photocatalysts and water splitting that turn sunlight and water into hydrogen and value-added chemicals.

04

Devices Beyond Solar

Memristors, photodetectors and simulation approaches that carry these materials from the lab toward real application.
